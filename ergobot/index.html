Ergo Bot Analysis

ErgoBot guides you step-by-step through the process to adjust the position of your desk and computer to help eliminate back, arm, shoulder and hand pain at work.

This personalized, self-guided program promotes fitness, health, posture, work breaks, and movement at your desk. Increase your energy and productivity at work, decrease your pain and discomfort!

It provides product suggestions with the option to purchase products. ErgoBot leads you through the proper adjustments to avoid work pain.
